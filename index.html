Our History

The Durham House of Pets (DHP) was founded in July of 2015 by a University of New Hampshire graduate, Jane Doe, who wanted to use her recently earned degree in Business Administration and her love for animals to open a business to benefit the overall community and the Durham area. The business started off very small, as only custom designed dog leashes were sold at first. Since then, the Durham House of Pets has expanded immensely and we now sell food for dogs, cats and fish, as well as the necessary supplies that are required in order to properly care for these three types of animals.
