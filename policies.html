Delivery Policy

At the Durham House of Pets, we proudly offer a delivery
service for items that you have pre-ordered over the
phone. We understand with the current circumstances
with COVID-19, many of our loyal customers feel more
comfortable with delivery services. We kindly ask that
you pay with a credit or debit card over the phone upon
delivery.

Return Policy

Our return policy is quite simple. If you are not pleased with one of our items that you have purchased, then you are able to return it under given circumstances. From the date of purchase, you have 20 days to return an item, if you are not pleased with it. Items should come back clean and in the same condition in which you bought them in. All animals foods are to NOT be returned once you have opened them.
